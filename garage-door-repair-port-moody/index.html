Broken Garage Door Spring Replacement in Port Moody

A snapped torsion spring is the #1 reason a garage door suddenly stops opening. If you heard a loud bang in your Port Moody garage and the door now feels extremely heavy or won't lift, you almost certainly have a broken spring. Don't try to force it open with the opener — you'll burn out the motor or strip the gears. Call us for same-day spring replacement anywhere in PoMo.

We replace torsion springs in matched pairs using high-cycle 25,000+ cycle oil-tempered springs backed by a lifetime warranty. Every spring replacement includes proper turns set to your door's exact weight, fresh centre bearing if needed, full balance test, and lubrication. Most Port Moody spring jobs are completed in 60–90 minutes the same day you call.

Garage Door Cable Replacement in Port Moody

Garage door lifting cables are a critical safety part that wear out from rust, fraying, and sudden drops after a spring failure. If a cable has come off the drum, you can see rust or broken strands, or the door is hanging crooked, the cables need to be replaced immediately. A snapped cable can cause the door to drop violently and damage panels or vehicles.

We replace garage door cables in pairs with new galvanized aircraft-grade lifting cables sized to your exact door height. Every cable replacement includes re-spooling both drums, re-tensioning the springs, rebalancing the door, and a full safety check. Most Port Moody cable jobs take 45–60 minutes on-site — an affordable repair that prevents far more expensive damage.

Off-Track Garage Door Repair in Port Moody

A garage door that has come off its track is an emergency. The door is unstable, panels can bend, and the opener can be seriously damaged if you keep cycling it. The most common causes in Port Moody are vehicle impact (catching the door on a bumper backing out), a snapped cable, broken rollers, or a loose track bracket. Whatever the cause, stop operating the door and call us right away.

Our off-track repair service includes safely re-seating the door, replacing damaged rollers, hinges, brackets, or cables, straightening or replacing bent track sections, realigning to spec, and a full balance and safety test. Most off-track jobs in Port Moody are resolved in a single same-day visit. Emergency service available evenings and weekends.

Garage Door Opener Motor Repair in Port Moody

If your garage door opener motor hums but the door doesn't move, makes a loud grinding noise, or runs only partway and stops, the internal drive gear is almost certainly stripped. We repair every brand of opener motor in Port Moody — LiftMaster, Chamberlain, Craftsman, Genie, Sears, and Sommer — with full gear-and-sprocket kits stocked on the truck for same-day service.

Our opener motor repair service includes diagnosis, removing the motor head, cleaning out old gear shavings, installing a new gear-and-sprocket assembly, re-greasing, and a full force/limit reset. About 45 minutes on-site. If your opener is over 12 years old we'll quote a fair comparison between repair and a new myQ smart opener — no pressure, no upselling.

Garage Door Hinge & Bracket Replacement

Cracked or broken hinges are a common cause of popping noises, panel separation, and doors that bind mid-travel. If your Port Moody garage door is making a loud snap when it transitions around the curve, scraping the track, or showing visible cracks at the panel joints, the hinges and roller brackets need to be replaced before the panels themselves get damaged.

We replace garage door hinges, end brackets, top brackets, and roller carriers with heavy-duty galvanized hardware on every type of sectional door. Hinge replacement is a fast, affordable repair that dramatically improves how the door feels and prevents far more expensive panel damage down the road. Most hinge jobs in Port Moody are completed in well under an hour.

Garage Door Roller Replacement — Quiet Door Upgrade

If your garage door is loud, grinding, or scraping every time it cycles, worn rollers are almost always the culprit. Old plastic and steel rollers wear out, lose their bearings, and start grinding against the track — loud doors that wake the kids, shake the house, and force the opener to fight friction. Replacing rollers is one of the highest-impact, lowest-cost upgrades on any Port Moody garage door.

We replace standard rollers with sealed nylon ball-bearing rollers that are dramatically quieter, smoother, and longer-lasting. Most Port Moody doors take 10–12 rollers and the full job is completed in about an hour. The difference is night and day — doors that used to wake the upstairs bedroom now open whisper-quiet.

Garage Door Preventive Maintenance & Tune-Up

An annual garage door tune-up is the cheapest insurance you can buy on the largest moving part of your home. Regular maintenance prevents broken springs, snapped cables, worn rollers, and unexpected breakdowns — and it's required to keep most manufacturer warranties valid. We offer affordable annual maintenance plans for Port Moody homeowners and property managers.

Our 21-point preventive maintenance service includes lubricating the springs, hinges, bearings, and rollers, tightening every nut and bolt, testing the safety reverse and photo eyes, balancing the door, checking cables and drums for wear, and flagging anything getting close to end-of-life. Most Port Moody homes are in and out in 45 minutes.
