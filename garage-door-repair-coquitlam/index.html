Broken Garage Door Spring Replacement in Coquitlam

A snapped garage door spring is the #1 reason a door suddenly stops opening. If you heard a loud bang in your Coquitlam garage and now the door feels impossibly heavy or won't budge, you almost certainly have a broken torsion spring. Don't try to force the door open with the opener — you'll burn out the motor or strip the gear. Call us and we'll be on-site fast.

We replace broken torsion springs the same day, in pairs, with high-cycle 25,000+ cycle oil-tempered springs that come with a lifetime warranty. Every replacement includes a fresh centre bearing if needed, proper turns set to your door's exact weight, full balance test, and lubrication of the new coils. Standard residential spring replacements in Coquitlam are typically completed in 60–90 minutes.

Heavy-Duty Torsion Springs for Wide & Custom Doors

Wide double doors, oversized custom doors, and heavy wood or full-view aluminum doors need more than the standard pair of springs. We install heavy-duty 4-spring (dual pair) torsion systems on Coquitlam estate homes, custom builds, and shop doors. Splitting the load across four springs cuts each spring's working stress in half — meaning quieter operation, smoother lift, and roughly double the service life before the next replacement.

Our heavy-duty spring service includes proper engineering of the wire size, inside diameter, and length to match your exact door weight, fresh centre and end bearings, new cables if worn, and a full balance test. Every install is backed by a lifetime warranty on the springs themselves. We service estate homes across Westwood Plateau, Burke Mountain, and Eagle Ridge.

LiftMaster Opener Chain Repair in Coquitlam

If your LiftMaster, Chamberlain, or Craftsman chain-drive opener is hanging slack, has thrown the chain off the sprocket, or the motor runs but the door doesn't move, we repair it the same day. Common causes are a stretched chain, a broken trolley, a stripped drive sprocket, or a snapped travel cable. We carry replacement chains, trolleys, sprockets, rails, and travel-cable kits for every LiftMaster Professional 1/2 HP, 3/4 HP, and 1 HP unit on the truck.

Our chain-drive repair service includes re-seating or replacing the chain, installing a new trolley if needed, re-tensioning to spec, lubrication, and a full force/limit reset. If your opener is 15+ years old, we'll give you an honest opinion on whether to repair or replace — we don't push new openers when a $150 repair will keep yours running for years.

Garage Door Cable Replacement in Coquitlam

Garage door lifting cables are a high-wear safety part. In our wet West Coast climate, cables on Coquitlam doors that face the weather often rust from the inside out, fray, and eventually snap — sometimes dropping the door violently. If you can see rust, broken strands, or one cable has come off the drum, stop using the door immediately and call us.

We replace garage door cables in pairs with new galvanized aircraft-grade lifting cables sized to your exact door height. Every cable replacement includes re-spooling the drums, re-tensioning the springs, rebalancing the door, and a full safety inspection. Most Coquitlam cable jobs take 45–60 minutes on-site — an affordable preventive fix that avoids a much more expensive emergency repair later.
