NEW GARAGE DOORS

Premium Doors Supplied & Installed — Coquitlam, Port Coquitlam & Port Moody

Transform Your Home's Curb Appeal

A new garage door is one of the highest-ROI home improvements you can make. At POCO Garage Doors, we supply and install premium doors from the industry's most trusted manufacturers, with options for every style, budget, and climate requirement.

Our Featured Brands & Models

Hormann ThermaTech

Model 3400 — Insulated

The Hormann 3400 ThermaTech is our most popular insulated door. Its 40mm thick steel-faced polyurethane core delivers outstanding thermal efficiency — perfect for attached garages in BC's variable climate. Available in a wide range of colours and panel designs.

R-value: ~17 | Best for: Attached garages, heated spaces, year-round comfort.

Hormann 500 Series

Non-Insulated

Reliable, cost-effective, and good-looking. The Hormann 500 Series is ideal for detached garages or situations where insulation isn't a priority. Durable steel construction in multiple finishes with minimal maintenance requirements.

Best for: Detached garages, workshops, budget-conscious homeowners.

Amarr Lincoln

Carriage House Style

The Amarr Lincoln is our most popular carriage-house style door. It brings classic charm with modern durability — steel construction with embossed wood-grain texture and full insulation. A standout choice for homes with traditional or craftsman architecture.

Best for: Curb appeal, traditional homes, upscale neighbourhoods.
