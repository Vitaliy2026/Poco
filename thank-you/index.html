While you’re here

Cheap insurance against a Saturday-morning emergency.

Nine out of ten broken-spring calls we run could have been avoided with a 30-minute tune-up the year before. We catch worn springs, dragging rollers and loose hardware before they leave you stuck in the driveway with the kids in the back seat.

  • ✓ Full torsion-spring & cable inspection
  • ✓ Hinge, roller & track lubrication
  • ✓ Opener force & safety-reverse calibration
  • ✓ Weather-seal & balance check
