Common Garage Door Opener Problems We Fix

Our certified technicians diagnose and repair every type of garage door opener malfunction — from worn-out gears and burned motors to faulty safety sensors and dead remotes. If your opener is acting up, we'll find the root cause and give you an honest, up-front quote before any work begins.

Opener Won't Open or Close

Dead motor, stripped gear, broken capacitor, or burned-out logic board — we diagnose and repair non-responsive openers fast.

Door Reverses Before Closing

Misaligned safety sensors, dirty photo eyes, broken springs, or incorrectly set travel limits are the usual culprits. We fix all four.

Loud Grinding or Humming

Worn drive gears, loose chains, dry rollers, or failing motor bearings cause noise. We replace LiftMaster and Chamberlain gear kits in under an hour.

Remote or Keypad Not Working

Dead batteries, programming issues, broken receivers, or worn-out wireless keypads. We reprogram and replace remotes for every brand.

MyQ & Wi-Fi Connection Issues

Smart opener won't connect to MyQ app, Wi-Fi keeps dropping, or notifications stopped working? We troubleshoot LiftMaster smart connectivity on every model.

Safety Sensor Errors

Blinking LED lights mean misaligned or faulty photo-eye safety sensors. We realign, rewire, or replace sensors to bring your opener back into compliance.

Broken Drive Belt or Chain

Snapped belt, stretched chain, or off-track trolley assembly. We carry replacement belts, chains, and trolleys for every LiftMaster model.

Battery Backup Failure

485LM battery alarm beeping or dead during a power outage? We replace LiftMaster battery backups and test the full charging system.

Logic Board / Circuit Board Repair

Power surges and lightning strikes fry circuit boards. We supply and install genuine LiftMaster and Chamberlain logic boards, often same-day.
